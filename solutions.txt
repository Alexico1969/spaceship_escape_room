SPACESHIP ESCAPE ROOM — SOLUTIONS
==================================

LEVEL 1 — Crew Quarters (code)
  look at desk       → PIN = locker batch, then pillow tag, then bunk number
  look at locker     → water bottle, batch number 7
  look at bunk       → pillow tag reads 4
  Description says   → bunk number 2
  Code: 7, 4, 2  →  742

LEVEL 2 — Storage Corridor (inventory)
  look at shelf      → magnetic keycard
  take magnetic keycard
  use magnetic keycard

LEVEL 3 — Airlock Antechamber (riddle)
  Answer: light

LEVEL 4 — Maintenance Bay (code)
  look at note       → override code = gauge readings left to right
  look at gauges     → 3, 1, 4, 1
  Code: 3141
  Also: take wrench  (needed for level 5)

LEVEL 5 — Coolant Control (combo)
  take pipe section
  combine wrench and pipe section   → fitted pipe
  use fitted pipe

LEVEL 6 — Power Relay (sequence)
  look at diagram    → power must flow: A then C
  switch a
  switch c

LEVEL 7 — Hydroponics Lab (code)
  look at terminal   → C=6, H=1, N=7, O=8
  look at note       → C then H then O then N
  Code: 6187

LEVEL 8 — Communications Array (riddle)
  look at star chart → Neptune, a distinct star sign, Venus, Antares
  First letters: N-O-V-A
  Answer: nova

LEVEL 9 — Captain's Quarters (combo + cipher)
  take cipher wheel
  take captain's log
  use cipher wheel 13 on log        → decoded log
  use decoded log

LEVEL 10 — Security Office (riddle)
  look at profiles   → Kai, Dex, Zara
  look at note       → Kai and Dex have been assigned
  Answer: zara

LEVEL 11 — Reactor Anteroom (code, reversed)
  look at legend         → Red=1, Yellow=3, Green=2, Blue=4
  look at warning panel  → sequence: Yellow, Red, Green, Blue, Red, Yellow
  Forward values:          3, 1, 2, 4, 1, 3
  Reversed:                3, 1, 4, 2, 1, 3
  Code: 314213

LEVEL 12 — Reactor Core (combo + gloves)
  open cabinet       → gloves
  take gloves
  take fuel rod      (gloves required — bare hands get burned)
  take containment sleeve
  combine fuel rod and containment sleeve   → contained fuel rod
  use contained fuel rod

LEVEL 13 — Life Support (riddle)
  Riddle: I have seas but no water, mountains but no stone,
          cities but no buildings.
  Answer: map

LEVEL 14 — Navigation Bay (code + observation)
  look at charts     → count stars: Orion=8, Lyra=4, Cygnus=9
  look at nav note   → code: Orion, Lyra, Cygnus, Σ
  Σ = 8 + 4 + 9 = 21
  Code: 84921

LEVEL 15 — Cargo Hold (code)
  look at classification key  → HZM = Hazardous Material
  look at manifest            → COOL 165kg HZM | FCELL 278kg HZM
  look at crane panel         → override = combined mass of all HZM units
  165 + 278 = 443
  Code: 443

LEVEL 16 — Fuel Bay (code, lockout after 5 attempts)
  look at display    → sequence: B, C, E, H, M, ?
  Fibonacci:           2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21
  Letters:             B, C, E, H, M, U
  Answer: u

LEVEL 17 — Escape Pod Prep Bay (code)
  fix locker with fitted pipe  (also accepts: wrench or multi-tool)
  look at emergency protocol   → load heaviest first,
                                  DOCK CODE = pack × load position
  look at crates               → Oxygen: pack 6, 82kg
                                  Rations: pack 4, 45kg
                                  Medical: pack 3, 31kg
  Heaviest first: Oxygen(pos1), Rations(pos2), Medical(pos3)
  6×1=6, 4×2=8, 3×3=9
  Code: 689
  take launch key   (required before exit)

LEVEL 18 — Systems Override (combo)
  take access card
  combine launch key and access card   → authorized key
  use authorized key

LEVEL 19 — Escape Pod Bay (retrieval)
  look at door       → key visible in keyhole
  take pencil        (from cabinet)
  slide log under door
  push pencil into keyhole   → key falls on decoded log
  pull log           → metal key retrieved
  use metal key      (or: open door with metal key)
